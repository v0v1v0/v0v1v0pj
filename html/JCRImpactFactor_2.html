JCRImpactFactor R Documentation

Journal Citation Reports (JCR) Impact Factor by Clarivate Analytics

Description

This package provides the Impact Factor of a journal reported by Journal Citation Reports (JCR) of Clarivate Analytics. The impact factor is provided for only those journals that are included Journal Citation Reports (JCR).
